The College

This axiom has been the bedrock on which stand the inception and the ensuing growth of this reputed institution, A. P. C. Mahalaxmi College for Women, Tuticorin. Fuelled by an innate urge to empower the illiterate masses of his under developed vicinity, and thereby, build up a strong nation with patriotic and socially responsible citizens on the Gandhian principles of peace, non violence and communal harmony, the institution was established by its Founder President, a well known philanthropist, and freedom fighter, Kulapathy Sri. A. P. C. Veerabahu.

Sports

The Physical Education programme develops leaders in physical education, coaching, recreation and sports management. We are committed to preparing students to meet complex challenges of our regional and global communities and to prepare the students for lifelong wellness. Our mission is to improve and increase the quality and quantity of high quality physical education for pupils, to show how physical education can enhance pupils’achievement and to create pathways for them to continue to be active beyond college. We will be developing our links to outside tournament and clubs which will help to generate positive interaction in the community.

Anti-Ragging

Ragging in any form, either in words or in action is a crime as per supreme court orders. In order to curb the menace of ragging in higher educational Institutions, the college has an active Anti-Ragging Cell functioning from and it ensures that no such offence takes place within the premises. Library

The library is equipped with an excellent collection of books, journals, e-journals and non-book materials in Science, Humanities, Management and General for the welfare of the students and the members of the staff. The library has a total collection of 29,514 volumes and also a good collection of reference materials, especially for Tamil literature. Books related to competitive exams are also available. Books are classified according to S.R.Ranganathan’s (Father of library Science) colon classification. It maintains a separate collection of donated books, bound volumes of journals, theses, etc., a modern digital library network with high speed internet and inflibnet facility (N-List e-resources) is also available in the library.

Health Club

Health club is formed with a aim of creating health awareness among students. The Health club provides periodical orientation on personal health, hygiene, awareness about seasonal diseases and preventive measures. Nearly 200 students are member of health club.

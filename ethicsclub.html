Ethics Club

Ethics club has been initiated with the motive of giving value based education to our students. It organizes sessions to inculcate discipline and culture among the students. Almost all the students of the college attend the sessions in turn.
Ethics club monitors the conduction of ethics classes to the final year students. This club plans to conduct several programmes on teaching the moral values of ethics and culture to our college students.

Objective:

  • To build a new generation with honest and strong ethical values.
  • To develop skills in articulating values.

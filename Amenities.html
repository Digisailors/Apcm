LIBRARY

We have an centralized library that is equipped with huge number of domestic, international books and magazines. Access hour: weekdays 8.30am to 4pm Borrowing & Renewing: 15 days Browsing available.

TECHNOLOGY THEATRE

We have 4 technology theatres with LCD projector and smart board facility. Video conferencing lectures have also been arranged.

LANGUAGE LAB

Language lab is provided with audio video facility with a capacity to hold an assembly of our sixty members.

Hostel

College hostel provides nutritious vegetarian food to nearly 100 students. Miss. Vasuki is to chief wardan.Other facilities in the hostel include, tv room, std booth, application for admission to hostel is to be made separately. Lodging and establishment changes are to be paid in advance in each term. The hostel in maids has to add here to the rules and regulations of the hostel, strictly.

CONFERENCE HALL

There is a compact air conditioned conference hall with a capacity to hold an assembly of about one hundred an fifty students where guest lectures, seminor are held.

LABORATORY

We have advanced laboratories in multiple disciplines including Computer Science , Physics , Chemistry , Zoology , Botony.

VEHICLE SHED

Separate vechicle shed is available to park the geared and nongeared vehicles.

CANTEEN

Canteen is functioning in the campus for the benefit of students and staffs. The enjoy appetizing quality and hygienic food in an extremely pleasand ambionce. Varieties of break fast, lunch bakery items are available.
